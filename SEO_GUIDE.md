### Step 2: Enable GitHub Pages

1. Go to your repository: https://github.com/ASIF-RIA/Portfulio
2. Click **"Settings"** tab
3. Scroll down to **"Pages"** section (in left sidebar)
4. Under **"Source"**, select:
   - **Branch**: `main`
   - **Folder**: `/ (root)`
5. Click **"Save"**
6. Wait 1-2 minutes for GitHub to build your site

Your portfolio will be live at:

## üîç Making Your Site Discoverable on Google

### 1. **Submit to Google Search Console** (FREE)

1. Go to [Google Search Console](https://search.google.com/search-console)
2. Sign in with your Google account
3. Click **"Add Property"**
4. Enter your URL: `https://asif-ria.github.io/Portfulio/`
5. Verify ownership (choose **HTML tag** method):
   - Copy the verification code
   - Add it to your `index.html` in the `<head>` section (if needed)
6. Submit your sitemap:
   - Go to **Sitemaps** section
   - Enter: `sitemap.xml`
   - Click **Submit**

### 2. **Submit to Other Search Engines**

- **Bing Webmaster Tools**: https://www.bing.com/webmasters
- Submit your sitemap URL: `https://asif-ria.github.io/Portfulio/sitemap.xml`

### 3. **Index Your Site Faster**

After setting up Google Search Console:
1. Go to **URL Inspection** tool
2. Enter your portfolio URL
3. Click **"Request Indexing"**
