# How to Find Your Portfolio in Google Chrome Search

## üöÄ Quick Steps to Make Your Portfolio Searchable

### Step 1: Enable GitHub Pages (Make Your Site Live)

1. Go to: **https://github.com/ASIF-RIA/Portfulio**
2. Click **"Settings"** tab (top right)
3. Scroll to **"Pages"** in left sidebar
4. Under **"Source"**, select:
   - Branch: **`main`**
   - Folder: **`/ (root)`**
5. Click **"Save"**
6. Wait 1-2 minutes

**Your site will be live at**: `https://asif-ria.github.io/Portfulio/`

---

### Step 2: Submit to Google Search Console (FREE)

This is the MOST IMPORTANT step to appear in Google search!

1. **Go to Google Search Console**: https://search.google.com/search-console
2. **Sign in** with your Google account
3. **Add Property**:
   - Click "Add Property"
   - Choose "URL prefix"
   - Enter: `https://asif-ria.github.io/Portfulio/`
   - Click "Continue"
4. **Verify Ownership**:
   - Choose "HTML tag" method (easiest)
   - Copy the verification code
   - We'll add it to your HTML (or use alternative verification)
   - OR use "URL prefix" verification if available
5. **Submit Sitemap**:
   - After verification, go to "Sitemaps" in left menu
   - Enter: `sitemap.xml`
   - Click "Submit"

---

### Step 3: Request Indexing

After setting up Search Console:

1. In Google Search Console, click **"URL Inspection"** (top search bar)
2. Enter: `https://asif-ria.github.io/Portfulio/`
3. Click **"Request Indexing"**
4. Wait for confirmation
