## Step 1: Enable GitHub Pages (5 minutes)

### Instructions:
1. **GitHub Pages Settings** (should be open in your browser)
   - URL: https://github.com/ASIF-RIA/Portfulio/settings/pages

2. **Configure Source:**
   - Under "Source", select:
     - **Branch**: `main`
     - **Folder**: `/ (root)`
   
3. **Click "Save"**

4. **Wait 1-2 minutes** for GitHub to build your site

5. **Your site will be live at:**
   ```
   https://asif-ria.github.io/Portfulio/
   ```

6. **Test it:** Open the URL above to verify it works!

## Step 2: Submit to Google Search Console (10 minutes)

### Instructions:
1. **Google Search Console** (should be open in your browser)
   - URL: https://search.google.com/search-console

2. **Add Property:**
   - Click **"Add Property"** button
   - Choose **"URL prefix"** option
   - Enter: `https://asif-ria.github.io/Portfulio/`
   - Click **"Continue"**

3. **Verify Ownership:**
   - Choose **"HTML tag"** method (easiest)
   - Copy the verification code (looks like: `<meta name="google-site-verification" content="..."/>`)
   - **OR** use **"URL prefix"** verification if available (even easier!)

4. **After Verification:**
   - Go to **"Sitemaps"** in the left menu
   - Enter: `sitemap.xml`
   - Click **"Submit"**

---

## Step 3: Request Indexing (2 minutes)

### Instructions:
1. In Google Search Console, click **"URL Inspection"** (top search bar)

2. Enter your portfolio URL:
   ```
   https://asif-ria.github.io/Portfulio/
   ```

3. Click **"Request Indexing"**

4. Wait for confirmation message
