## ğŸ” Step 2: Make Your Site Discoverable on Google (FREE)

### A. Submit to Google Search Console

1. **Go to Google Search Console**: https://search.google.com/search-console
2. **Sign in** with your Google account
3. **Add Property**:
   - Click "Add Property"
   - Enter: `https://asif-ria.github.io/Portfulio/`
   - Click "Continue"
4. **Verify Ownership** (choose HTML tag method):
   - Copy the verification meta tag provided
   - Or use the **"URL prefix"** verification method (easier)
5. **Submit Sitemap**:
   - After verification, go to **"Sitemaps"** in left menu
   - Enter: `sitemap.xml`
   - Click **"Submit"**

### B. Submit to Bing Webmaster Tools (Optional)

1. Go to: https://www.bing.com/webmasters
2. Sign in with Microsoft account
3. Add your site: `https://asif-ria.github.io/Portfulio/`
4. Submit your sitemap: `https://asif-ria.github.io/Portfulio/sitemap.xml`

### C. Request Indexing

After setting up Google Search Console:
1. Use **"URL Inspection"** tool
2. Enter your portfolio URL
3. Click **"Request Indexing"**
